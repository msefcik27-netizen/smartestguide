About us

Built for hoteliers,
by people who travel

SmartestGuide started with a simple frustration: why do guests still have to call reception to ask when breakfast ends, where the parking is, or what's worth seeing nearby? Every hotel has this information — it just isn't available at midnight from a guest's phone. Printed flyers run out. Reception can't always speak every guest's language.

We built a system that reads your website, learns your hotel, and becomes available to guests in seconds — via a QR code that works in any browser, in any language.

We're a small team based in the Czech Republic, focused on the European hospitality market.
